The Wow! Signal

August 15, 1977 - A Cosmic Mystery

The Wow! Signal was a strong narrowband radio signal detected by the Big Ear radio telescope at Ohio State University. It lasted for 72 seconds and has never been detected again.

The signal's characteristics matched those predicted for an interstellar communication, coming from the direction of the constellation Sagittarius. Astronomer Jerry R. Ehman circled the signal's code on the computer printout and wrote "Wow!" next to it, giving the signal its name.

Despite numerous follow-up attempts, the signal has never been re-detected, and its origin remains unexplained. It continues to be one of the most intriguing events in SETI (Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence) research history.
